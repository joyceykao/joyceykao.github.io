I'm Joyce.

I grew up in the United States (Texas to be exact - yee haw!). Having lived in Los Angeles and New York and spending many years in front of the camera, getting behind the lens was just a natural extension of my aesthetic hobbies. Now located in Switzerland, I enjoy collaborating with artists on this side of the world. Photography is one part technical thinking about lighting, angles, camera settings; one part creativity coming up with original concepts; and one part communication to effectively work together to realize the final vision.

Most of my portfolio comes from my travels and requests from friends and colleagues, but I also am open to making new acquaintances so get in touch today.

Aside from photography, I enjoy developing websites from code to content and finding hidden gems in the Swiss countryside with my family.
